This Potato, Kale and Pecorino Frittata is courtesy of In Sock Monkey Slippers as part of the U.S. Potato Board's Potato Lovers Club.
This Potato Frittata is an easy way to freshen up a weeknight dinner.

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil over medium heat in a 10-inch ovenproof and nonstick skillet
  3. Add the remaining 1 tablespoon oil to the skillet and add the garlic and kale and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Once the frittata is finished, run a knife around the edge of the skillet and gently slide the frittata onto a carving board or serving plate.

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided

  • 1 clove garlic, minced
